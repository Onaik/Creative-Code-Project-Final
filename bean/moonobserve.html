Any genuine attempt to understand the flat earth theory would find that the concepts do not fall in line with the basic laws of our reality

Beyond all the technology that has been built upon our spherical situation, practical observations of 3 Dimensional shapes would show us that the theory can not stand on its own or provide any scientific merit.



If the moon were close above us and we stood on a disk, we would be seeing different faces of the moon from different countries at the same time of day.

In reality, both objects are tidally locked, and we always see the same face of the moon.
